General Gameplay

Every assassin is assigned a unique word and a target. To kill their target, the assassin must get them to say the assigned word aloud

Rules

  1. Your target must say the word exactly as it appears, no variations tolerated
  2. Each assassin must be directly responsible for getting their target to say the word. If your target says the word during conversation, it only counts if it was directly in response to a question you asked
  3. Your target must say the word aloud. No writing, email or slack
  4. Keep your target and word secret!!
